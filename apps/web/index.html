FAQ

Questions, answered.

Will this fix my Google Ads conversions after the Consent Mode v2 cutoff?

Yes. Tagshield sets all four Consent Mode v2 signals to safe defaults before any tag fires and updates them on the visitor's choice, so Google Ads and GA4 receive accurate consent signals.

Is it really under 10KB?

The banner runtime ships with a CI size gate that fails the build above 10KB gzipped. It's async and edge-delivered, so it doesn't move your Core Web Vitals.

Do you charge per domain?

No. Pricing is flat, per monthly unique visitor — every site you run is under one bill. Agencies get pooled visitors across client workspaces.

What is the consent vault?

Every consent decision is appended to a hash-chained, tamper-evident log and anchored daily. You can export a signed proof bundle and verify the chain yourself.

Are you a Google-certified CMP?

We're Google Consent Mode v2 native and on the certification path. We never claim certification we don't have, and Tagshield is a tool, not legal advice.
